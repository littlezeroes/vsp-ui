Mo hinh: MoMo + Revolut + Monzo | MVP Recommended

Uu diem

  • Auto-detect can logic phuc tap (SDT vs STK overlap)
  • User moi co the khong biet co the nhap STK vao cung o search
  • 3 lane khong explicit — user phai "kham pha"

Option C: Contacts-First

Mo hinh: WeChat Pay + Venmo + Zelle
Vi VSP: tap contact co vi Bank: link "Chuyen bang STK" Search: filter danh ba + da luu

Danh ba la trung tam — full contact list, badge ai co vi VSP. "Da luu" = pinned section tren cung (ca P2P + bank). Tap contact → neu co VSP → chuyen luon. Neu khong → hoi nhap STK. Link "Chuyen bang STK" o bottom cho bank transfer khong qua contact.

Uu diem
  • Contact-centric: phu hop mental model "gui cho NGUOI"

  • Social proof: thay ai dung VSP → viral growth
  • Bank transfer van co, chi khong la primary
Nhuoc diem
  • Bank transfer bi "secondary" — user bank phai scroll
  • Phu thuoc contact sync permission
  • VSP user base nho → danh ba toan nguoi khong dung VSP
